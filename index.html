Welcome to the Brainstorming Network 🧠

This platform uses an embedded AI model to build an interconnected web of our team's concepts.


How to use it:
  • Submit: Add your concept via the sidebar.
  • AI Connections: The engine reads your text and automatically connects it to other nodes that share semantic similarity.
  • Vote & Discuss: Click any idea to Upvote it (which makes it visually grow) or add Comments.
  • Navigate: The graph will automatically organize and lock into place. Use the >< button to manually gather scattered nodes!
